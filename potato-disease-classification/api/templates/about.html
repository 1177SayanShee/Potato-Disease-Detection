ABOUT US

Plant Disease Detector is a web-based application designed to help farmers, botanists, and agriculture enthusiasts quickly identify plant diseases using machine learning.

Simply upload a photo of a leaf, and our system will analyze it to predict the disease and suggest possible remedies.

This project aims to promote sustainable agriculture by reducing crop loss and improving early diagnosis of plant health issues.
